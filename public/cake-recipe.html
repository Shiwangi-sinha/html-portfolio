Shiwangi's Recipe for the Best Cake

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3 cup all purpose flour
  • 11/2 cup powddered sugar
  • 4 egg
  • 1/2 cup virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla essense
  • Mixing bowl
  • Vanilla extract and electric mixer

Make whipping cream:

  • Mix heavy whipping cream, powdered sugar with some vanilla extract in the bowl.
  • Whip them all together at medium-high speed.
  • Make sure to stop in between and check the consistency of whipping cream.
  • Once done, transfer it in a cone as well to decorate your cake.

Instructions

  1. Cream together oil and sugar and then blend with beaten eggs.
  2. Combine flour mix and beaten eggs.
  3. Bake the cake as per your convenience.
  4. Check with a knife or skewer if it is cooked and serve.
